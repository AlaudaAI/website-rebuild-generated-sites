Contact Kious, Rodgers, Barger & Sanderson (KRBS)

Our law offices are located downtown on Maple Street in the historic John C. Spence House, just off the historic square in Murfreesboro, TN, and one block from the Rutherford County Judicial Center. The house bears the name of its original owner, John Cedric Spence, a prominent nineteenth century Murfreesboro businessman, industrialist and chronicler of county history, and has been the home of the KRBS law offices since 2003.
