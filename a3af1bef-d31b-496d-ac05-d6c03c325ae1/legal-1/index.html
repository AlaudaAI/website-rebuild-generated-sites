About KRBS

Kious, Rodgers, Barger & Sanderson, PLLC (KRBS)

Represents businesses and individuals in Murfreesboro and throughout Middle Tennessee with a commitment to providing sound legal advice and exceptional personal service. Our team of attorneys is composed of skilled professionals with more than 75 years of collective experience in the boardroom and the courtroom. Backed by a high-caliber legal support team, KRBS provides the experience you need and the results you want.

Legal Services

Areas of Practice

Whether in the courtroom or boardroom, our diverse client base ranges from individuals and small businesses to some of the largest companies in Middle Tennessee.

Business Law

Commercial litigation, breach of contract, business dissolution, trade secrets, trademark infringement, and franchises

Company Formation

Establishment of corporations, Limited Liability Companies (LLC), Limited Liability Partnerships and other partnerships

Employment Law

Employment agreements, employee termination and wrongful termination, worker's compensation, non-competition agreements, and discrimination

Estate Planning

Wills, Powers of Attorney, Trust formation, estate planning and probate process

Family Law

Divorce, child custody, child support, contempt, dependent neglect, and adoption

Divorce

Contested and uncontested divorce, child custody, visitation rights, relocation, alimony, and child support

Child Custody

Parenting plans, visitation rights, relocation, and child support calculations

Personal Injury

Motor vehicle accidents, worker compensation, defective products, falls, wrongful death, head and spinal injury, railroad accidents

Probate

Settling estates, distribution of assets and resolution of claims filed against estates

Real Estate Law

Commercial and residential real estate, purchase agreements, leases, loan documents, liens, restrictive covenants, land use, zoning, subdivision

Social Security Disability

Appeals for denied Social Security disability claims and representation at hearings before administrative law judges

Mediation

Family law dispute mediation and co-mediation services

Criminal Defense
