At Innate Beauty, all medical cosmetic procedures are performed by our Physician Artist, Dr. Shirat Ling, a Board-certified family physician specializing in cosmetic medicine and aesthetic procedures. Our focus is on helping people heal themselves and rediscover their inherent beauty with natural-appearing non-surgical enhancements.

We provide non-invasive and minimally-invasive medical cosmetic procedures, such as the CO2 fractional laser, platelet-rich plasma, Botox, dermal fillers (Juvederm, Voluma, Sculptra, Radiesse, Restylane, Belotero), Intense Pulsed Light (photofacials, skin tightening, acne treatments, spider vein and hair removal), microdermabrasion, Dermapen microneedling, dermaplaning, chemical peels, Kybella, mesotherapy spot fat dissolving, body contouring with TruSculpt and Contourlight, and professional teeth whitening. Our goal is to recreate your own younger self with natural-appearing enhancements and reasonable pricing.
