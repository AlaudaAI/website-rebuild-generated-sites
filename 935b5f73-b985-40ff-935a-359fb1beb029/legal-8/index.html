Our Legal Services

Edwards & Edwards provides comprehensive legal services with decades of experience serving clients throughout the region.

Wills and Probate

Assisting clients in the preparation of wills, powers of attorney, living wills, and trusts. Edwards & Edwards also represent clients in probate court proceedings, including probating estates and conservatorships.

Family Law

Handling family law matters, including divorce, custody, support, adoptions, guardianship proceedings, and prenuptial agreements, Edwards & Edwards has experience in complex divorce cases involving a large amount of assets and business valuations.

Personal Injury

Representing clients in personal injury matters, including motor vehicle accidents, wrongful death, and premises liability, including representation of a client in a wrongful death case that went all the way to the Supreme Court.

Business Law

Edwards & Edwards represents businesses, both large and small, in their formation, litigation, and collections. Their litigation experience in this area includes complicated business disputes to simple collection of debts.

Meet Our Attorneys

Experienced legal professionals dedicated to serving Sumner County and surrounding areas.

Russ Edwards has been with the Edwards & Edwards since 1998. He was admitted to the Tennessee Bar in 2003 after graduating from the Nashville School of Law. Russ is the Hendersonville City Judge (part time), was a Hendersonville City Alderman, and is very active in the community, serving on various boards of directors.

Russ Edwards

Attorney

Mike Edwards has been practicing law almost exclusively in Sumner County since 1974. After graduating in the top ten of his class from the YMCA Night Law School (currently the Nashville School of Law), he was admitted to the Tennessee Bar in 1974. Prior to law school, Michael earned his bachelor's degree in accounting from Belmont University.

Mike Edwards

Attorney

William Foster has been with Edwards & Edwards since 2025. A graduate of the University of Mississippi School of Law, William obtained his license to practice law in the State of Tennessee in 2025. Before attending law school in the Magnolia State, William attended Auburn University where he earned his bachelor's degree in business administration.
