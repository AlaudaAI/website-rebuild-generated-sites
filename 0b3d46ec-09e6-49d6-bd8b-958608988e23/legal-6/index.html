Our lawyers have been serving the areas of Middle Tennessee since 1993. Since then, we have served thousands of clients in the areas of personal injury, criminal defense, and other areas requiring litigation experience.

Every legal strategy is custom tailored to our client's needs.

Walwyn & Walwyn has built a strong practice based upon determination, attention to detail, and a reputation forwinning. Our firm is very proud of winning cases where previous attorneys have given up.

You or a loved one should not have to suffer and pay for another persons negligence. Many times accidents will present long term negative effects that don't present right away. The lawyers at Walwyn & Walwyn understand your rights and how insurance companies will try to pressue your to settle for sums that won't cover your short or long term bills. Don't let them bully you into make a quick decision. Our attorneys will stand up and fight for your rights. We will push to get the settlement you deserve so you are not left with a heavy financial load to carry due to someone else's lack of concern or attention.

If you have suffered the death of a loved one caused by an accident, or if you have been badly injured, wrongly accused, or simply in need of courtroom lawyers willing to fight for you, Walwyn & Walwyn stands ready to help. Put us to work for you today.
