Real Estate Law

Real estate legal matters are of the essence in today's residential and commercial market

The attorneys at KRBS represent clients in a wide variety of real estate matters both residential and commercial. In fact, we have been involved in some of the largest real estate transactions in the Middle Tennessee area.

Our attorneys are experienced in drafting and reviewing purchase and sale agreements, residential and commercial leases, loan documents, liens, and restrictive covenants. We are also experienced in land use, zoning, subdivision, and other land development issues, as well as litigation of real estate related conflicts such as construction liens, mechanic's and/or materialman's liens, condemnations and title disputes.
