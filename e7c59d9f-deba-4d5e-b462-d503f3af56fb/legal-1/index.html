About KRBS Law

Kious, Rodgers, Barger & Sanderson, PLLC (KRBS)

Represents businesses and individuals in Murfreesboro and throughout Middle Tennessee with a commitment to providing sound legal advice and exceptional personal service. Our team of attorneys is composed of skilled professionals with more than 75 years of collective experience in the boardroom and the courtroom. Backed by a high-caliber legal support team, KRBS provides the experience you need and the results you want.

Our Practice Areas

Areas of Practice

Whether in the courtroom or boardroom, our diverse client base ranges from individuals and small businesses to some of the largest companies in Middle Tennessee.

Business Law

Corporate and business clients in disputes involving breach of contract, unfair competition, partners and shareholders, business dissolution, trade secrets, directors' and officers' liability, trademark infringement, and franchises

Company Formation

Establishment of organized entities including Corporations, Limited Liability Companies (LLC), Limited Liability Partnerships and other Partnerships

Employment Law

Employment agreements, employee termination and wrongful termination, worker's compensation, non-competition agreements, and discrimination

Estate Planning

Protecting your assets and ensuring that your property is distributed according to your wishes, including charitable giving

Family Law

Divorce, child custody, child support, contempt, dependent neglect, and adoption

Divorce

Contested and uncontested divorce proceedings with experience in complex financial situations

Child Custody

Child custody battles, visitation rights, relocation, and child support matters

Personal Injury

Motor vehicle accidents, work accidents, defective products, falls, wrongful death, head and spinal injury, railroad accidents

Probate

Settling the estate of a deceased person, including distribution of assets and resolution of claims

Real Estate Law

Commercial and residential real estate transactions, purchase agreements, leases, loan documents, liens, restrictive covenants, land use, zoning, subdivision

Social Security Disability

Assistance with Social Security Disability claims, appeals, and hearings before administrative law judges

Mediation

Family law dispute mediation services with Rule 31 Listed Civil Mediator

Criminal Defense
Insurance Defense
