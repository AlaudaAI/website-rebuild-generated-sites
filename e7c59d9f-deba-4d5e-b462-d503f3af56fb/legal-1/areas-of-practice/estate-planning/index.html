Estate Planning

Protect your assets with an attentive approach from a dedicated Middle Tennessee estate planning attorney

Estate Planning is the process of Protecting your assets and ensuring that your property is distributed according to your wishes, including charitable giving.

Whether it be constructing a simple Will or creating a complex estate planning strategy, our attorneys will expertly guide you through the disposition of your personal affairs, including the preparing of Powers of Attorney, the formation of a legal Trust, estate planning and the probate process.
