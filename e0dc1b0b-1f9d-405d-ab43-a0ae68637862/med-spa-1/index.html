Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking, and policies.

What treatments do you offer?

At Spa Éclat, we specialize in advanced, results-driven skincare treatments including Hydrafacials, customized facials, Nano Needling and targeted treatments designed to improve your skin's health and glow. Every service is tailored to your unique skin needs.

Which Hydrafacial should I choose?

If you're unsure which Hydrafacial is right for you, here's a simple guide:

The Signature Hydrafacial is ideal for clients with low-maintenance, problem-free skin who are looking to maintain a healthy glow.

The Deluxe Hydrafacial is perfect for those wanting to target specific skin concerns, as it includes personalized boosters tailored to your skin, partnered with relaxing and rejuvenating Blue or Red LED light therapy.

The Platinum Hydrafacial is designed for clients who want a complete skin health reset, combining detoxifying lymphatic drainage, a personalized booster to target specific skin concerns, and Blue or Red LED light therapy for a more comprehensive, results-driven experience.

Is there any downtime after a facial?

Our treatments have no downtime. You can usually return to your daily activities right after your appointment, with your skin looking refreshed, hydrated, and glowing.

How often should I get a facial?

For best results, we recommend a facial every 4 weeks. Consistency helps maintain healthy, balanced skin and enhances long-term results.

How can I book an appointment?

Appointments can be booked easily through our website or by contacting us directly. We recommend booking in advance to secure your preferred time.
