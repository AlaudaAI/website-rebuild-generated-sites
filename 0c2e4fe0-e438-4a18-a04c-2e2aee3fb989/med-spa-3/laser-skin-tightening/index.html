    Pre & Post Treatment Care

    At Truly Lifted Medspa, we want you to get the best possible results from your Laser Skin Tightening treatment. Following these simple pre- and post-care guidelines will help maximize your comfort and ensure optimal skin rejuvenation.

    Pre-Treatment Care

    • Avoid excessive sun exposure and tanning beds for at least 2 weeks before treatment.
    • Stop using self-tanning products 2 weeks before your session.
    • Avoid cosmetic treatments (fillers, Botox, microneedling, peels, lasers) in the treatment area unless cleared by your provider.
    • Discontinue retinols, tretinoin, and AHA/BHA products 5–7 days prior.
    • Arrive with clean skin—no makeup, perfumes, lotions, or cosmetic products.
    • Remove all jewelry, including earrings, necklaces, and piercings, before your session.
    • Shave the treatment area 1 day before if needed.
    • Stay well-hydrated for several days before treatment to support optimal skin response.

    Post-Treatment Care

    • Expect minimal to no downtime; redness or swelling typically subsides within 24 hours.
    • Apply a broad-spectrum SPF 30 or higher daily and avoid direct sun exposure for 3–5 days.
    • Resume wearing makeup after 24 hours if desired.
    • Keep your skin hydrated and moisturized with gentle, soothing products.
    • Avoid chemical peels, intense facials, or aggressive skincare for at least 1 week.

    By following these guidelines, you'll enjoy a smoother recovery and see the full benefits of your treatment sooner.
