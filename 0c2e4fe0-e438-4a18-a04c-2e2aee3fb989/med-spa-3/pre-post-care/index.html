    • Pre/Post Care

    01

    Dermal Fillers (Juvederm, Restylane)

    Pre-Treatment Care:

    • Avoid alcohol 48 hours before treatment
    • Don't take NSAIDs (Aspirin, Motrin, Advil, Ibuprofen) 7 days before unless otherwise medically directed
    • Start Arnica 3-7 days prior to reduce bruising and swelling
    • Stop Omega 3 & Fish Oil supplements 7 days prior
    • Avoid Vitamin E supplements 7 days before
    • Reschedule if you have fever, cold, flu or cold sores/shingles
    • Pregnant/breastfeeding patients cannot be treated
    • Consider scheduling 2 weeks before any big events
    02

    Dermal Fillers (Juvederm, Restylane)

    Post-Treatment Care:

    • Apply ice/cold compress as needed for comfort and to reduce swelling
    • Avoid alcohol for 48 hours
    • Sleep upright for 1st night if possible
    • Avoid strenuous exercise for 48 hours
    • Avoid extreme heat/cold (saunas, hot tubs, etc.) for 2 weeks
    • Gentle makeup application 4 hours after treatment
    • Massage gently only if instructed to do so
    • Bruising/swelling may occur and can last up to 2 weeks
    • Results are immediate but may take 2 weeks to settle
    • Call if you have concerns about infection, severe swelling, or vascular compromise
    03

    Neurotoxin (Dysport, Jeuveau, Botox)

    Pre-Treatment Care:

    • Avoid NSAIDs like Advil®, Ibuprofen®, and Aspirin® for 14 days to reduce bruising risk. Always consult your prescribing physician before stopping any medication.
    • Refrain from alcohol consumption 48 hours before treatment.
    • Begin taking oral Arnica 7-10 days prior to minimize bruising.
    • Deodorant can be worn if treated for excessive sweating.
    • Pregnant or breastfeeding women cannot receive treatment.
    04

    Neurotoxin (Dysport, Jeuveau, Botox)

    Post-Treatment Care:

    • Multiple procedures can be done the same day if scheduled accordingly.
    • Avoid strenuous activities and manipulating injection sites for 24 hours.
    • Do not lie flat for 4 hours post-treatment to ensure optimal results.
    • Bruising may occur but should resolve within 14 days.
    • Allow 10-14 days for the full effect of the treatment to set in.
    • Deodorant can be resumed if treated for excessive sweating.
    05

    Skinpen Microneedling

    Pre-Treatment Care:

    • Avoid facial irritants such as retinol, glycolic acid, and Vitamin C for 7 days before.
    • Reschedule if the skin is irritated and inform your provider about skin conditions.
    • Postpone treatment if experiencing cold sores, fever blisters, or shingles.
    • Ensure men are clean-shaven 24 hours beforehand.
    06

    Skinpen Microneedling

    Post-Treatment Care:

    • Keep skin well-moisturized with recommended products for 24 hours.
    • Post-procedure, the skin might resemble a sunburn but will improve in a day.
    • Avoid exercise for 48 hours to prevent irritation.
    • Normal skincare can resume after 3-5 days, avoiding potent ingredients.
    • Manage any flaking gently and refrain from picking the skin.
    • Use mineral makeup after 24-48 hours and apply SPF 30 after the first 48 hours.
