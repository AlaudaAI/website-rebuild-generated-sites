    What Are Dermal Fillers?

    Dermal fillers are injectable substances used to restore volume, smooth lines and wrinkles, and enhance facial contours. They are typically made from hyaluronic acid, a naturally occurring substance in the body that helps maintain skin hydration and elasticity.

    Dermal fillers are a popular non-surgical cosmetic treatment option for rejuvenating the face and achieving a more youthful look. If needles aren't your preference, consider our chemical peels for skin rejuvenation without the need for injections.

    Benefits Of Dermal Fillers

    Smooths Wrinkles

    Dermal fillers effectively reduce fine lines and wrinkles, providing smoother, more youthful-looking skin by restoring lost volume and promoting collagen production.

    Restores Volume

    They can restore lost volume in the main areas such as cheeks, temples, and under the eyes, enhancing facial contours for a more youthful appearance.

    Minimizes Scars

    Dermal fillers reduce the appearance of scars, such as acne scars, by filling in depressions and smoothing the skin's surface, resulting in a more even skin tone.

    Restylane Lyft

    Restylane Lyft is a hyaluronic acid-based dermal filler designed to add volume and fullness to the cheeks and correct age-related midface volume loss. It is also FDA-approved for hand augmentation to correct volume loss in the back of the hands. The unique formulation of Restylane Lyft provides long-lasting results, with studies showing that the effects can last up to 12 months in the cheeks and up to 6 months in the hands.

    The treatment is performed by injecting the filler into targeted areas, and the results are immediate, with minimal downtime. Restylane Lyft is a safe and effective option for individuals looking to restore volume and achieve a more youthful appearance in the cheeks and hands.

    Juvederm Ultra XC

    Juvederm Ultra XC is a hyaluronic acid-based dermal filler designed to add volume and smoothness to the lips and perioral area. It is FDA-approved for lip augmentation and the correction of moderate to severe facial wrinkles and folds, such as nasolabial folds. The unique formulation of Juvederm Ultra XC includes lidocaine, a local anesthetic, to enhance patient comfort during the injection process.

    The results are immediate, providing natural-looking enhancement and definition to the lips. The effects of Juvederm Ultra XC can last up to one year, making it a long-lasting option for lip augmentation and facial rejuvenation. It is a safe and effective treatment performed by trained healthcare providers to help patients achieve their desired lip enhancement goals.

    Sculptra

    Unique dermal filler made from poly-L-lactic acid that stimulates collagen production for natural-looking, long-lasting results. It is designed to restore facial structure and volume lost due to aging and collagen depletion. Unlike traditional fillers that provide immediate volume, Sculptra works gradually over several months to improve skin texture and increase collagen production, resulting in natural-looking, long-lasting results.

    The effects of Sculptra can last up to two years, making it a popular choice for clients seeking a more youthful appearance without the obvious signs of having had cosmetic work done. It is an ideal option for those looking for subtle yet significant improvements in facial volume and contours.

    Are dermal filler treatments painful?

    Dermal filler treatments are generally well-tolerated, with minimal discomfort for most patients. This is because many fillers contain a local anesthetic, such as lidocaine, which helps numb the treatment area and reduce any potential pain. While some individuals may experience mild discomfort or a stinging sensation during the injection process, it is usually brief and tolerable.

    Your healthcare provider can also apply a topical numbing cream before the procedure to further minimize any discomfort. Overall, the discomfort associated with dermal filler treatments is typically mild and temporary, and most patients find the procedure to be well worth the results.

    Are there any side effects of dermal fillers?

    Common side effects of dermal fillers include temporary swelling, bruising, redness, and tenderness at the injection sites, which typically resolve within a few days. These effects are often mild and can be managed with ice packs and over-the-counter pain relievers.

    In rare cases, more serious side effects such as infection, granulomas (lumps under the skin), or allergic reactions may occur. It's important to discuss any concerns with your healthcare provider and to follow their post-treatment care instructions carefully to minimize the risk of complications.

    What areas can be treated with dermal fillers?

    Dermal fillers are versatile and can enhance various facial areas. They are commonly used to add volume and definition to the lips, cheeks, and jawline, smoothing out nasolabial folds and marionette lines. Fillers can also be applied to the temples to restore volume and improve facial symmetry.

    Additionally, they can address under-eye hollows, reducing the appearance of dark circles and hollow areas. The precise placement and amount of filler used depend on individual needs and desired outcomes, and a skilled provider can tailor the treatment to achieve natural-looking results.
