    EZPRF

    PRF (Platelet-Rich Fibrin) is an autologous blood concentrate, meaning it is made using your own blood to stimulate natural healing and rejuvenation.

    It contains a high concentration of platelets, white blood cells, and fibrin, which work together to support tissue regeneration, collagen production, and skin revitalization—without the use of additives or anticoagulants. How? We isolate your own regenerative cells using a slow and short centrifugal ion process. These cells will then be used for skin improvement purposes.

    EZGEL

    EZGEL is a 100% natural, injectable biostimulant created from your blood using the EZPRF system. It combines your own powerful proteins with heat-activated albumin to form a gel-like consistency, offering a volumizing and regenerative alternative to synthetic fillers.

    EZGEL not only provides a subtle lift but also promotes long-term skin rejuvenation by encouraging your body's natural collagen production.

    Pre-Care Instructions

    • Hydration: Stay hydrated for at least 72 hours before treatment for ease of blood draw.
    • Medication: Avoid blood thinners and NSAIDs.
    • Alcohol: Do not consume alcoholic beverages for at least 24 hours prior.
    • Diet: Avoid a high triglyceride (high fat) diet.
    • Smoking: Refrain from smoking for maximum treatment efficacy.

    Post-Care Instructions

    • Cooling: Avoid applying ice or any cold compresses to the treatment area.
    • Contact: Do not touch or massage the treatment area.
    • Swelling: Swelling is normal in the days following treatment.
    • Medication Consultation: Consult your healthcare provider before taking ibuprofen or aspirin.

    FAQs

    Navigate your beauty journey seamlessly with answers to common queries in our PRF & EZ gel treatment FAQs section.

    What is EZPRF and EZGEL?

    EZPRF: PRF (Platelet-Rich Fibrin) is an autologous blood concentrate made from your own blood, containing a high concentration of platelets, white blood cells, and fibrin. It supports tissue regeneration, collagen production, and skin revitalization without additives or anticoagulants.

    EZGEL: A 100% natural, injectable biostimulant created from your blood using the EZPRF system. It combines your own proteins with heat-activated albumin to form a gel-like consistency, offering a volumizing and regenerative alternative to synthetic fillers.

    What is the difference between PRP and PRF?

    PRF (Platelet-Rich Fibrin) is a second-generation concentrate that doesn't require additives or anticoagulants. It utilizes the latest technologies to maximize the body's natural healing response without environmental damage, unlike PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma), which often requires additives to prevent clotting.

    What is the EZPRF procedure like?
    1. A small sample of your blood is collected in the EzPRF tubes.
    2. The tubes are spun in the centrifuge to separate your red blood cells.
    3. Collect PRF blood concentrate composed of your platelets and white blood cells.
    What is the EZGEL procedure like?
    1. Blood draw.
    2. Prepare albumin-rich plasma using the centrifuge and EZ Heat device, and prepare PRF using the centrifuge and EZcool device.
    3. Prepare EZGel blood concentrate by mixing albumin-rich plasma with PRF prior to injection.
