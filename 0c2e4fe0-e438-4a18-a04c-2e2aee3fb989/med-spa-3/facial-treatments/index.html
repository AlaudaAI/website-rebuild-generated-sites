    FAQs

    Navigate your beauty journey seamlessly with answers to common queries in our FAQs section.

    Are there any side effects of HydraFacial?

    While side effects of HydraFacial are rare, they may include temporary redness, tightness, or mild irritation, which typically resolve within a few hours. These are usually mild and transient, and most people can resume their normal activities immediately after the treatment.

    It's important to follow post-care instructions provided by your skincare professional to ensure optimal results and minimize any potential side effects. If you experience any persistent or severe side effects, it's best to consult with your skincare provider for further evaluation and guidance.

    Can I wear makeup after a Dermaplane treatment?

    After a Dermaplane treatment, it is generally recommended to avoid wearing makeup for the rest of the day to allow your skin to heal and breathe. Makeup can potentially clog your pores or irritate your skin, hindering the healing process.

    By giving your skin a makeup-free break, you allow it to fully absorb the benefits of the treatment, promoting smoother and healthier-looking skin. It's a small sacrifice for the long-term benefits of Dermaplane, ensuring that your skin receives the care and attention it needs to thrive.

    How soon can I see results after an Microtox Infusion Facial?

    You may begin to notice initial improvements in your skin's texture and appearance within a few days after an Microtox Infusion Facial. These immediate results can include a smoother, more radiant complexion. Over the following weeks, as your skin continues to respond to the treatment, you can expect to see further enhancements in skin tone, texture, and overall quality.

    The long-term benefits of an Microtox Infusion Facial, such as reduced fine lines, improved elasticity, and enhanced hydration, become more pronounced with each subsequent treatment, leading to a sustained improvement in your skin's health and vitality.
