    NAD+ Therapy for Renewed Vitality and Wellness

    NAD+ therapy is a cutting-edge treatment that focuses on enhancing your body's natural ability to repair and rejuvenate at the cellular level. This therapy involves administering NAD+ (nicotinamide adenine dinucleotide), a vital coenzyme found in every cell. It plays a crucial role in energy production and metabolic processes.

    Our NAD+ therapy sessions are tailored to each individual, ensuring you receive the precise support your body needs. The treatment includes a comprehensive consultation to understand your unique wellness goals and requirements, followed by customized NAD+ administration under expert supervision.

    Ideal candidates for NAD+ therapy are individuals looking to boost overall wellness and improve cellular function. Whether you're seeking better mental clarity or enhanced vitality, NAD+ therapy provides a personalized approach to rejuvenating your well-being.

    The NAD+ therapy process begins with a personalized consultation to assess your health goals and needs. Our experts design a customized plan to ensure optimal results, aligning the treatment with your specific wellness objectives.

    Pre & Post Treatment Care

    Pre-Treatment Care

    Before your NAD+ therapy session, ensure you're well-hydrated and have eaten a light meal. Avoid alcohol and strenuous activity for at least 24 hours prior. If you're taking any medications, inform your provider to rule out potential interactions.

    Is NAD+ therapy safe for all age groups?
    NAD+ therapy is generally safe for healthy adults when administered by qualified professionals, but it is not recommended for all age groups. Clinical research on its safety in children, pregnant or breastfeeding women, and older adults with certain medical conditions is limited. Therefore, NAD+ therapy should be approached cautiously in these populations. Prior to receiving treatment, a comprehensive health assessment and consultation are essential to identify any potential risks or contraindications. Always consult with a trusted healthcare provider to determine if NAD+ therapy is appropriate for your specific age group and individual health profile to ensure safety.
    How soon can I expect to feel the effects?
    The timeframe for experiencing the effects of NAD+ therapy can vary from person to person. Some individuals report feeling improvements in energy, mental clarity, or overall well-being within a few hours to days after their initial session. Others may notice more gradual changes over several sessions as NAD+ levels build up in the body. Factors such as your general health, metabolism, lifestyle, and specific wellness goals all play a role in how quickly you may see benefits. Consulting with your provider will help set realistic expectations and determine the optimal treatment plan for noticeable results.
    How long does each NAD+ therapy session take?
    Each NAD+ therapy session typically lasts between one to two hours, depending on the personalized treatment plan and the dosage administered. The therapy is conducted in a comfortable setting, allowing you to relax during the infusion. Our team ensures a seamless experience, making the session both productive and rejuvenating while accommodating your schedule.
