    FAQs

    Navigate your beauty journey seamlessly with answers to common queries in our FAQs section.

    Can I buy the AnteAGE Exosomes serum for at-home use?

    No, the AnteAGE Exosomes serum is designed to be applied professionally as part of our microneedling service. The expertise of a trained professional ensures the serum is correctly administered for maximum effectiveness and safety. DIY application can result in suboptimal outcomes and potentially harm your skin or hair. It's best reserved for in-office treatments to guarantee quality results.

    What aftercare is recommended following microneedling with AnteAGE Exosomes?

    After your microneedling treatment with AnteAGE Exosomes, it's crucial to follow a gentle skincare routine. Avoid direct sun exposure, and use a broad-spectrum SPF to protect your skin. Refrain from using exfoliants or active ingredients like retinol for a few days post-treatment. Keep the treated area clean and hydrated with recommended products to aid in healing and enhance results.

    Is microneedling with AnteAGE Exosomes suitable for summertime?

    Yes, microneedling with AnteAGE Exosomes can be performed during summertime, but extra precautions should be taken. It's important to apply a high-quality, broad-spectrum sunscreen daily to protect your skin from UV rays. Avoid prolonged sun exposure and avoid activities that may expose your treated skin to direct sunlight for extended periods. Wearing protective clothing and a wide-brimmed hat can also help protect your skin as it heals and rejuvenates. Proper sun protection ensures you get the best results while minimizing the risk of adverse effects.
