About Us

Truly Lifted Medspa is a sanctuary where innovation meets excellence, offering personalized, advanced aesthetic and wellness services to enhance your natural beauty and well-being.

Our Journey: How Truly Lifted Medspa Came to Be

Charisse Lin, the founder of Truly Lifted Medspa, traces her journey in the aesthetics industry back to her mother's work at Marie France in the Philippines, where they focused on body contouring and facial aesthetics. Witnessing the clients' happiness and transformations sparked Charisse's passion for aesthetics. As she pursued her nursing career, she realized her deep interest in this evolving field.

During her clinical rotations as an FNP student, Charisse had the privilege of learning from one of the city's top dermatologists, whose mentorship instilled in her the confidence to pursue dermatology. Over the past five years, Charisse has garnered extensive experience at a high-end medical spa in Austin, specializing in skincare, injectables and body treatments.

Now, as she launches Truly Lifted Medspa, Charisse is excited to bring her expertise to Georgetown, close to the city of Round Rock, TX and the surrounding region. With her education, passion, and experience, Charisse is dedicated to providing the highest quality aesthetic and wellness care to all her clients.

Founder, Aesthetic Nurse Practitioner

Once I began my nursing career, I realized that I had a deep passion for aesthetics and could eventually specialize in this ever-growing field.
