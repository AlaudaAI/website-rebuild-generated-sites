Welcome to Aubrey Givens & Associates, PLLC, your trusted personal injury law firm in Madison, Tennessee. With a commitment to providing exceptional legal services, we proudly serve clients throughout the Nashville area. Our team of dedicated attorneys is here to assist you with all of your legal needs.

Established in 2001, Aubrey Givens & Associates, PLLC is a full-service law firm that truly cares about helping individuals who have been injured in car accidents or are in need of assistance with Social Security disability claims. We understand the impact that these situations can have on your life, and we are here to provide compassionate support and top-quality representation.

With offices in Nashville, Memphis and Lebanon, Tennessee, our attorneys are readily available to handle your legal matters, no matter where you are located. Whether your case is in Memphis, Nashville, Cookeville, Clarksville, Johnson City, or anywhere in between, we are prepared to go wherever you need us to go.
