Professional Legal Services

At the law firm of E. Evan Cope, PLLC, we handle a wide variety of matters on behalf of a diverse group of clients. We represent businesses, local government and individuals in matters ranging from litigation in both state and federal courts, contract negotiation, advocacy before government boards and agencies and general legal advice on matters of importance to our clients.

Many of the cases our firm handles are complex and have serious ramifications – there is often a lot at stake. We are committed to providing the very best advocacy and advice as we guide our clients through high-stakes and emotionally charged legal issues with the attention and care they deserve.
