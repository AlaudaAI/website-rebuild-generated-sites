Welcome to Daydreaming Spa, Austin's premier destination for head spa and skincare services. Among our locations, Daydreaming Head Spa—conveniently situated in North Austin—specializes in the art of Japanese-style head spa treatments, featuring our signature ASMR scalp shampoo massage.

Our skincare offerings are equally exceptional, with treatments including chemical peels, acne care, anti-aging therapies, oxygen and hydration facials, back facials, and soothing facial massages.

At Daydreaming, we blend innovation with relaxation to elevate your beauty and wellness journey.
