Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a divorce take in Smyrna, TN?

The divorce process varies depending on the case. Uncontested divorces may finish in a few months, while cases involving disputes over property, custody, or support may take longer to resolve.

Does Tennessee require separation before filing for divorce?

No formal separation is required, but at least one spouse must have lived in Tennessee for six months before filing for divorce in Smyrna.

What is the difference between contested and uncontested divorce?

Uncontested divorces occur when both parties agree on major issues. Contested divorces involve unresolved disputes, which may require mediation or a court hearing to resolve.

How does the court handle property division?

Tennessee follows equitable distribution, so courts divide marital property fairly, though not always equally, considering many factors unique to each marriage.

Will I need to go to court during my divorce?

Many divorces settle outside of court through negotiation or mediation, but some cases require a hearing before a judge, especially if disputes remain unresolved.
