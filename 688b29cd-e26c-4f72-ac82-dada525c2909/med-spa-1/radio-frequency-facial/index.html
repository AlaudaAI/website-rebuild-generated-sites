Radio Frequency Facials are non-invasive, non-surgical treatments that use radio frequency energy to lift, firm, and tighten the skin, leaving you with a more youthful and rejuvenated appearance.

Benefits of Radio Frequency FacialS

A radio frequency facial is an excellent treatment for those who want to reduce the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles and sagging skin. It has numerous benefits, including:

  • Stimulating collagen production, which leads to tighter and firmer skin.
  • Reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.
  • Improving skin texture and tone.
  • Minimizing the appearance of pores.
  • Non-invasive and pain-free.

Treatment Type

A radio frequency facial is a non-invasive treatment that uses radio frequency energy to heat the deeper layers of your skin. The heat causes your skin to contract, which leads to tighter and firmer skin. The treatment can be customized to target specific areas, such as the cheeks, forehead, neck, and jawline.

Who's a good candidate?

Radio frequency facials are suitable for people of all skin types and ages who are looking to improve the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, and sagging skin. It is especially beneficial for those who are not yet ready for more invasive treatments, such as facelifts or surgery.

What to expect during the procedure

During the procedure, a handheld device will be used to deliver radio frequency energy to your skin. You may feel a warm sensation as the energy penetrates your skin, but it is not painful. The treatment typically takes between 30 and 60 minutes, depending on the area being treated.

What to expect after the treatment

After the treatment, you may experience some mild redness or swelling, but this should subside within a few hours. There is no downtime, and you can resume your normal activities immediately after the treatment.

Recovery time

There is no recovery time required after a radio frequency facial. You can resume your normal activities immediately after the treatment.

Do's and Don'ts Before and After the Procedure

Before the procedure:

  • Avoid sun exposure for at least two weeks before the procedure
  • Do not use any retinoids or exfoliants for at least one week before the treatment.
  • If you have any skin conditions or allergies, inform your provider before the treatment.

After the procedure:

  • Avoid direct sun exposure for at least two weeks after the treatment.
  • Do not use any retinoids or exfoliants for at least one week after the treatment.
  • Use a gentle cleanser and moisturizer on your skin.
  • Drink plenty of water to stay hydrated.
