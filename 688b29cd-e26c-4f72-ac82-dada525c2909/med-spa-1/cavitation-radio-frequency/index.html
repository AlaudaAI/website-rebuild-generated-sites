Benefits of Cavitation & Radio Frequency

  • Fat Reduction: Cavitation utilizes ultrasonic waves to break down stubborn fat cells, which are then naturally eliminated by the body. Radio Frequency, on the other hand, stimulates collagen production, tightening loose skin and reducing cellulite.
  • Non-Invasive: These treatments are non-surgical and require no incisions, making them an excellent alternative to traditional liposuction or surgical skin tightening procedures.
  • Safe and Effective: Cavitation and Radio Frequency treatments have been proven to be safe and effective in reducing fat and improving skin tightness, with minimal discomfort and downtime.
  • Body Contouring: These procedures can target specific areas such as the abdomen, thighs, arms, love handles, and buttocks, allowing for precise body contouring and sculpting.

Treatment Types

  • Cavitation: During a Cavitation treatment, a handheld device emits low-frequency ultrasound waves that penetrate the skin, selectively targeting fat cells. The energy disrupts the fat cell membrane, causing it to release its contents, which are then naturally eliminated by the body's lymphatic system.

  • Radio Frequency: Radio Frequency treatment uses radiofrequency energy to heat the deeper layers of the skin, stimulating collagen production and tightening loose skin. This process improves skin elasticity, reduces wrinkles, and enhances overall skin tone.

Ideal Candidates

Cavitation and Radio Frequency treatments are suitable for individuals who:

  • Have localized pockets of stubborn fat that are resistant to diet and exercise.

  • Desire skin tightening and cellulite reduction.

  • Are in overall good health and have realistic expectations.

  • Are committed to maintaining a healthy lifestyle after the treatment.

During the Procedure

  • Consultation: You'll have an initial consultation with a qualified practitioner who will assess your areas of concern, discuss your goals, and determine if you're a suitable candidate for the treatment.
  • Preparation: Before the procedure, your practitioner may apply a conductive gel to the treatment area. You may also be asked to remove any jewelry or clothing that could interfere with the treatment.
  • Treatment: During the session, the handheld device will be applied to the targeted area. For Cavitation, you may feel a gentle warming sensation or mild vibrations. Radio Frequency treatment is often described as a warm, comfortable sensation on the skin.
  • Duration: The duration of each session can vary depending on the size and number of areas being treated but typically ranges from 30 minutes to an hour.

After the Treatment

  • Immediate Results: After the procedure, you may notice an immediate reduction in the treated area's circumference and improved skin tightness. However, full results will gradually appear over the following weeks as your body eliminates the released fat cells and collagen production increases.
  • No Downtime: Cavitation and Radio Frequency treatments are non-invasive, allowing you to resume your normal activities immediately after the session. You may experience temporary redness, mild swelling, or a warm sensation in the treated area, which typically subsides within a few hours.

Do's and Don'ts Before and After the Procedure

Before the procedure

  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water.
  • Avoid excessive sun exposure or tanning beds.
  • Inform your practitioner about any medications or supplements you are taking.
  • Shave or trim the treatment area, if necessary, to optimize contact with the device.

After the procedure

  • Maintain a healthy diet and exercise regimen to optimize and prolong results.
  • Avoid exposing the treated area to excessive heat or extreme cold.
  • Protect your skin from the sun by using sunscreen and covering up.
  • Follow any specific aftercare instructions provided by your practitioner.
