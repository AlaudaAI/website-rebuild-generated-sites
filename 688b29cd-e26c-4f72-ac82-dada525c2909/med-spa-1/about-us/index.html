About Us

MONICA ALAMILLO

Meet the radiant heart of our med spa, Skin Therapist Extraordinaire, Bella Grace. With over a decade of experience illuminating the beauty industry, Bella has honed her therapeutic craft to an exact science, providing nothing short of spectacular results for her clients.

A Southern California native, Bella embarked on her aesthetic journey at the esteemed Stanford University, where she graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Biomedical Sciences. Intrigued by the healing power of nature on the human body, Bella's thesis on the impact of botanical extracts on skin health was ground-breaking and it eventually paved her way into the skincare industry.

After completing her degree, Bella attended the renowned American Academy of Dermatology, where she specialized in aesthetic skincare. During her tenure, she developed a deep interest in skin biology, with a particular focus on non-invasive treatments to enhance natural beauty.

In 2015, Bella joined our Med Spa, bringing her knowledge and passion to the forefront of our operations. Here, she has curated a suite of personalized treatments tailored to individual skin needs. Bella's belief in a holistic approach shines through in her use of bio-compatible products and techniques, proving that nature and science can work in harmony to achieve vibrant, healthy skin.

Bella's signature treatments, including her innovative 'Glow & Restore Facial', have become much sought after, attracting a broad clientele who swear by her transformative results. Bella is also highly skilled in advanced treatments such as laser resurfacing, chemical peels, and dermaplaning, offering a wide array of options to those looking for rejuvenated and refreshed skin.

As a firm believer in continual education, Bella regularly attends seminars and workshops to stay abreast of the latest trends and innovations in the industry. This commitment to her craft ensures she brings the latest and most effective treatments to her clients.

Meet Bella Grace – she's not just a skin therapist; she's a custodian of natural beauty, a believer in the healing power of nature, and the provider of radiant confidence that comes from within. With Bella, your skin is not just in good hands; it's in the best hands.
