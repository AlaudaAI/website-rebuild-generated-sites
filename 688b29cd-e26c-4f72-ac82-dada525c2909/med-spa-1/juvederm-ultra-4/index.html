Benefits of Juvederm Ultra 4

Juvederm Ultra 4 can provide numerous benefits, including:

  • Smoothing out deep wrinkles and folds
  • Restoring lost volume in areas such as the cheeks, temples, and chin
  • Enhancing facial contours and definition
  • Providing a more youthful, refreshed appearance
  • Long-lasting results that can last up to 12-18 months

Treatment Type

Juvederm Ultra 4 is a non-surgical cosmetic treatment that injects a smooth gel into the skin. The gel is made of hyaluronic acid, a naturally occurring substance that helps hydrate and plump up the skin. The treatment is minimally invasive and typically takes around 30 minutes to complete.

Who's a good candidate?

Good candidates for Juvederm Ultra 4 treatment include those looking to add volume and fullness to their cheeks, chin, or temples or those who want to fill in deep wrinkles and folds around the mouth, nose, and forehead. It is important to consult with a licensed and experienced healthcare provider to determine if Juvederm Ultra 4 is the right treatment for your specific concerns and goals. Pregnant and/or breastfeeding patients should not receive Juvederm Ultra 4 injections.

What to expect during the procedure

During the Juvederm Ultra 4 procedure, a trained medical professional will use a fine needle to inject the gel into the targeted areas of your face. The procedure is generally well-tolerated and relatively painless, but a local anesthetic cream or injection may be used to minimize any discomfort.

What to expect after the treatment

After the Juvederm Ultra 4 procedure, patients can expect some swelling, bruising, and redness around the injection site. These side effects are usually mild and should resolve within a few days. Patients can resume their normal activities immediately after treatment, but it is recommended to avoid strenuous exercise, sun exposure, and alcohol consumption for 24-48 hours after the procedure.

Recovery time

Following the Juvederm Ultra 4 treatment, you may experience some redness, swelling, or bruising at the injection sites. These side effects are typically mild and should subside within a few days. Most people are able to resume their normal activities immediately after the procedure.

Do's and Don'ts Before and After the Procedure

To ensure the best possible results and minimize any potential complications, there are some important do's and don'ts to keep in mind before and after your Juvederm Ultra 4 treatment:

Before the procedure:

  • Avoid taking aspirin, ibuprofen, or any other blood-thinning medications for at least a week before the procedure
  • Avoid alcohol and smoking for at least 24 hours before the treatment
  • Inform your medical professional if you have any allergies, medical conditions, or are taking any medications

After the procedure:

  • Avoid touching or massaging the treated areas for at least 24 hours after the procedure
  • Avoid strenuous exercise, exposure to extreme heat or cold, and excessive sun exposure for at least 24 hours after the treatment
  • Use a cold compress to reduce swelling and bruising if necessary
  • Follow any additional instructions provided by your medical professional
