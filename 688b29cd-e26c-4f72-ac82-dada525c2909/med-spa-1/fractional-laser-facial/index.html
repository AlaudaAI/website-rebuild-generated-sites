Who's a good candidate?

Fractional Laser Facial Treatments are suitable for anyone looking to improve the appearance of their skin. It is particularly effective for those with fine lines and wrinkles, acne scars, sun damage, and uneven skin tone.

What to expect during the procedure

Before the procedure, a numbing cream will be applied to the skin to minimize any discomfort. During the procedure, the laser will be directed at the skin, creating microscopic channels. The procedure typically takes around 30 minutes to an hour, depending on the size of the treatment area.

What to expect after the treatment

After the treatment, you may experience some redness and swelling in the treatment area. This is normal and should subside within a few days. You may also experience some peeling or flaking of the skin as it begins to heal.

Recovery time

Most patients are able to resume their normal activities immediately after the treatment. However, it is important to avoid direct sunlight and to use a high-quality sunscreen to protect the skin during the healing process.

Do's and Don'ts Before and After the Procedure

Before the procedure:

  • Avoid sun exposure for at least two weeks before the procedure
  • Avoid using any products that may irritate the skin, such as retinoids or glycolic acid
  • Avoid smoking or drinking alcohol

After the procedure:

  • Avoid direct sunlight and wear a high-quality sunscreen
  • Avoid using any products that may irritate the skin, such as retinoids or glycolic acid
  • Avoid smoking or drinking alcohol
  • Avoid swimming or soaking in hot tubs for at least two weeks after the procedure
