About Our Firm

Multi-State Domestic Relations Lawyers

Through a continued focus on client service and exclusive dedication to domestic relations law, Stange Law Firm has grown into a multi-state firm with an expanding national presence. Our attorneys handle a wide range of matters, offering clients focused experience in these complex and often emotional cases.

The firm's insights on domestic law issues have also been recognized by national media outlets, including CNBC, Cosmopolitan, NPR, Univision, MarketWatch, Yahoo! Entertainment, Investment News, and others. These features reflect the firm's broader role in discussing trends and developments on a national level.

Practice Area 01

Domestic Relations

We handle only domestic relations and related matters, which enables us to provide in-depth knowledge to address even the most complex, sophisticated, and serious issues for our clients.

Practice Area 02

Support

We are focused on protecting the rights of our clients while ensuring that child & spousal support is fairly and accurately calculated.

Practice Area 03

Dissolution of Marriage

We are here to help clients rebuild their lives while working through the difficult times and the challenges that come with dissolution and separation.

Practice Area 04

Property Division

Married for years with significant holdings or a shorter term marriage and less assets, we are well-prepared to achieve the best possible results when it comes to dividing property.

Practice Area 05

Parenting Time/Visitation

We place a great deal of emphasis on seeking parenting time, visitation, and allocation of parental responsibility solutions that protect and foster the valuable parent-child relationship.

Practice Area 06

Estate Planning

Estate planning is not just for the wealthy or those nearing retirement. It's for anyone looking for extra life protection, regardless of their financial situation – it's never too early to start.

Practice Area 07

Domestic Violence

We provide compassionate service & assertive representation for individuals seeking protections from abusers or others facing false allegations of abuse.

Practice Area 08

High Net Worth

We have extensive experience handling high-asset, complex, and challenging dissolution of marriage cases, as well as complex property division.

Practice Area 09

Alternative Dispute Resolution
