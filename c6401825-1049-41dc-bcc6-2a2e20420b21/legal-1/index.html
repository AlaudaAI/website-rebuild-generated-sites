Our Vision

Hamilton Gorham & Duncan is an association of attorneys committed to community.

We are located in the Bellevue community of Nashville, Tennessee. We live here, work here and play here and we're incredibly committed to the people of this area. Our commitment to the community can be seen in our involvement with civic organizations and other volunteer opportunities. It's this type of passion for people that carries into our practice.

Our community commitment means that we stake the claim of being the Bellevue community's preeminent source for legal services. However, our team of experienced attorneys represent all clients, corporate or individual, throughout all of Nashville, Davidson County and surrounding counties.
