About the Practice

Committed to Achieving the Best Outcome for Clients

Thomas H. Bray understands that effective representation requires a lawyer to be accessible to clients and responsive to their needs. He works closely with clients to explain the law and procedures while developing a legal strategy for their case. He believes accessibility and regular communication with clients is crucial to legal representation. A solid understanding of the law and the litigation process is key when your assets, time with your children, or your freedom are on the line.

Practice Area 01

Divorce & Family Law

Represents clients in both contested and uncontested divorces as well as post-divorce modifications and enforcement of alimony and support. Family law focus includes Adoptions, Paternity Actions, Guardianships, Grandparent's Rights, child custody battles, parenting plan modification and child support matters.

Practice Area 02

Criminal Defense

Experience handling defense cases for both misdemeanor and felony charges in General Sessions Criminal Court, Circuit Criminal Court and the Criminal Division of the Court of Appeals. From DUI to Domestic Assault.

Practice Area 03

Personal Injury

Personal injury cases with years of experience in front of Judges in Rutherford and the surrounding counties.
