002 — our approach

Face to Face Spa is a beauty med spa in Austin, TX, that blends relaxation with advanced, medically guided skin care. From the moment you arrive, you will notice a calm, welcoming atmosphere that encourages you to slow down and focus on yourself. Every appointment begins with a genuine conversation about your goals so your treatment plan feels customized, purposeful, and suited to your comfort level.

Clients come to Face to Face Spa for a consistent source of high-performance care and everyday rejuvenation. Whether you are refreshing your complexion, enhancing your lashes, or building a skin care routine that supports long-term wellness, each service is designed to fit naturally into your lifestyle. Many guests visit regularly because they enjoy how balanced, radiant, and confident they feel after each experience.

Schedule a personal consultation to discuss your skin care goals and discover which treatments are right for you. Our team will create a customized plan that fits your lifestyle and budget.

Botox
Smooth out forehead lines, crow's feet, and frown lines with this popular injectable. Results can last up to four months with little to no downtime.
Medi-Facials
These are not your average spa facials. Each one targets specific concerns with specialized active ingredients and advanced therapeutic techniques.
HydraFacials
These powerful treatments cleanse, exfoliate, and hydrate all in one session. Great for those who want an instant glow with no downtime.
Chemical Peels
These treatments stimulate new skin growth by removing the top layer of skin. Great for reducing fine lines and improving brightness.
Skin Rejuvenation
Turn back the clock on texture, tone, and elasticity. Our advanced skin rejuvenation options help you look refreshed, not overdone.
SkinPen Micro-needling
This treatment uses tiny, controlled pinpricks to encourage collagen and elastin production. Ideal for smoothing fine lines, wrinkles, and acne scars.
Dermaplaning
Get that glassy skin look by removing peach fuzz and dead skin. Makeup goes on smoother, and your face feels fresh and clean.
Microdermabrasion
Buff away dead skin and improve texture with this tried-and-true method. It's perfect for smoothing out rough patches or acne scars.
Lashes
Get fuller, longer-looking lashes with synthetic extensions. Perfect for adding drama to your everyday look.
Facial Contouring Fillers
Enhance your natural angles with strategic filler placement. Think sculpted cheeks, defined jawlines, and subtle lift—without surgery.
SkinVive
