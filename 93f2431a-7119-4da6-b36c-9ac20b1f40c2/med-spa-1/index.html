Services

Deep Tissue Massage

Deep Tissue Massage targets inner muscle layers to release chronic tension. Using slower strokes and firm, direct pressure, this technique breaks down stiffness and painful "knots." It is ideal for relieving specific issues like stiff necks or lower back pain, helping you move more freely.

Traditional Thai Massage

Traditional Thai Massage is an active therapy often described as "assisted yoga." Performed while you remain fully clothed, the therapist uses rhythmic compression and gentle stretching to improve your flexibility. This leaves you feeling energized and aligned without the use of oils or lotions.

Couples Massage

Couples Massage lets you share a relaxing experience side-by-side with a partner, friend, or family member. Two therapists work simultaneously in a private room, tailoring each person's massage to their specific needs. It is the perfect way to bond while you unwind together.

Cupping Therapy

Cupping Therapy uses special cups to create suction on the skin, increasing blood flow to relieve deep tension and promote repair. This technique is excellent for reducing pain and stiffness. Please note that temporary circular marks may appear as a sign of released stagnation and natural healing.

Four Hands Massage

Four Hands Massage is a luxurious experience where two therapists work simultaneously with synchronized movements. The harmony of four hands helps your mind completely let go, offering a deeper level of relaxation than a standard massage. It is designed to disconnect you from stress and provide double the relief.
