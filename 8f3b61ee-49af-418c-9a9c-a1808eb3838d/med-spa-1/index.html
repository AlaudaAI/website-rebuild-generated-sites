Welcome to Gorg! Wellness & Aesthetics, where confidence, self-care, and top-tier medical expertise come together in a fun, welcoming environment. Our Medspa is more than just a place for treatments—it's a space where you can relax, refresh, and embrace a rejuvenated version of yourself.

Whether you're looking for anti-aging solutions, skin rejuvenation, weight management, or wellness treatments, we're here to help you feel your absolute best. At Gorg!, we make aesthetics approachable, breaking away from the typical med spa experience that sometimes feels intimidating or overly clinical.

Gorg! Wellness & Aesthetics is a fun & fabulous local medspa here in Austin, Texas. We are proudly LGBTQ+ and Veteran-owned. Established in 2023, we set out with the mission to help as many people look and feel Gorg! as possible.
