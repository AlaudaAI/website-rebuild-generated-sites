Divorce & Post-Divorce Enforcement

The divorce process can take many shapes and forms from amicable and simple to complex and contested, and once a divorce is over, sometimes new issues arise. We will educate you on all the process options based on your unique circumstances.

Collaborative Divorce

Would you like to ensure you avoid a courtroom? Might a team that includes legal, financial, and mental health professionals be beneficial to your situation? The Collaborative Law Divorce process might be right for you.

Child Custody and Parentage

We know the relationship you have with your child or children is the most important in the world. Understanding how to navigate legal disputes that affect your children is critical. We are here to advocate, educate, and provide the support you need.

LGBTQ+ Family Law

All our firm members are advocates and allies for the LGBTQ+ community and we have community members within the firm. We know there is fear about the rapidly changing laws. We are here to support and help with the nuanced legal challenges you may face.

Prenuptial Agreements

Marriage changes the status of property ownership, whether or not you know about it or agree to it. Pre- and post-nuptial agreements take decision-making regarding how property is to be treated away from the state legislature and give it to the couple.

Mediation

Parties can use a mediator while having no attorneys involved, or with each party having counsel. Mediation can resolve disputes early without the need for litigation keeping costs and conflict down. We have several trained mediators at the firm.

Adoption

Welcoming a new family member is the greatest joy, but the steps to get there can feel daunting. We are here to help you realize your dream through careful navigation of the legal process.
