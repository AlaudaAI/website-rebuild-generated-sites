Common Questions

what should I expect during my first visit?

Your first visit includes a comprehensive consultation where we discuss your aesthetic goals, medical history, and treatment options. We'll create a personalized plan that fits your budget and timeline.

how long do results typically last?

Results vary depending on the treatment. Botox typically lasts 3-4 months, while lip fillers can last 6-12 months. We'll discuss expected duration during your consultation.

is there any downtime after treatments?

Most treatments have minimal to no downtime. You may experience slight swelling or bruising, but this typically resolves within a few days. We provide detailed aftercare instructions.

what makes your studio different?

Our boutique approach focuses on one-on-one care in a private setting. We prioritize natural results, transparency, and building long-term relationships with our clients.
