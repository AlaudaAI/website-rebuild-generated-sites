About Brad H. Frakes

If you are facing a divorce, child custody, child support, or family law matter, Brad H. Frakes, Attorney at Law, will fight for you. Serving families and individuals in Nashville and throughout middle Tennessee since 2012, he is dedicated to protecting our clients and ensuring that justice is served. Hard times can happen to anyone, and when they do, Brad vows to provide aggressive and compassionate representation.

Our practice areas include family law issues such as divorce, child custody, child support, alimony, orders of protection and adoption, as well as many others.
