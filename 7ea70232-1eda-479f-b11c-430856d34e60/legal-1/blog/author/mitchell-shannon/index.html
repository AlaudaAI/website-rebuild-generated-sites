Mitchell Shannon is the Founding Attorney of Boss Law, based in Murfreesboro, Tennessee. He has over 25 years of experience representing clients in family law, criminal law, and personal injury matters. His work includes divorce, child custody, domestic violence, and criminal proceedings. Mr. Shannon earned his Juris Doctor from Nashville School of Law and was admitted to the Tennessee Bar in 2001. He has received the Moot Court Award and the Richard F. Laroche, Sr. Pro Bono Award for his service and advocacy.
