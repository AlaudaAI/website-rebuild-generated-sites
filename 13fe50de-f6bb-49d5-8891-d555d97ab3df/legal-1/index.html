Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Baumgardner Law offer in Nashville, TN?

Baumgardner Law provides comprehensive family law services, including both contested and uncontested divorce, prenuptial and postnuptial agreements, order of protection matters, child custody and parentage matters, including child support issues, and related legal matters. Our Nashville-based firm guides clients through the legal process with personalized strategies to protect their rights and interests.

What is an uncontested divorce, and can Baumgardner Law help?

An uncontested divorce occurs when both parties agree on key issues such as property division, child custody, and support. Baumgardner Law guides clients through the divorce process, from the initial filing through the final hearing, ensuring a smooth process with minimal court involvement.

How is an uncontested divorce different from a contested divorce in Nashville?

While uncontested divorces involve mutual agreement on key issues, generally making the process faster and less costly, contested divorces involve disputes that may require mediation and/or court intervention.

What documents or requirements are needed to start a divorce case?

Typical requirements include statistical marital information, including dates of marriage and separation, financial records, documents related to property, and any relevant custody agreements. Baumgardner Law will provide a checklist during the consultation to help streamline the preparation process.

How much does a divorce law attorney in Nashville, TN charge?

Fees vary based on the complexity of each case. Baumgardner Law offers transparent pricing and will discuss payment options during the initial consultation. Contact us for your personalized estimate.

How quickly can I get divorced in Tennessee?

Tennessee has a statutory waiting period of 60 days if the parties have no minor children, or 90 days if the parties do have minor children. The statutory waiting period begins the day the Complaint for Divorce is filed. Contact Baumgardner Law to begin drafting your Complaint.

Does Baumgardner Law handle high-conflict divorce cases?

Yes, Baumgardner Law has experience managing complex and high-conflict divorces, including those in which domestic violence is present. Baumgardner Law ensures professional guidance throughout the entire legal process.

What is involved in child custody cases?

Baumgardner Law assists clients with custody agreements, modifications, visitation rights, establishing paternity, and enforcement. The focus of child custody cases remains aligned with the child's best interests, while the attorney advocates for the client's parental rights.
