002 — Stephanie Moreno, NP & Brittney Maxwell, LVN

Brittney Maxwell, LVN, and Stephanie Moreno, NP, are the co-founders of LUXE LEAGUE WELLNESS, bringing nearly two decades of combined hands-on nursing experience to the practice. Brittney's career began in 2008, evolving from caregiver and phlebotomist to licensed nurse, while Stephanie advanced from LVN in 2013 to board-certified Nurse Practitioner in 2022.

United by a passion for compassionate, evidence-based care and aesthetics, they created LUXE LEAGUE WELLNESS to offer personalized, trustworthy services that enhance confidence, wellness, and natural beauty. Outside the clinic, Brittney enjoys life with her three children and partner, and Stephanie cherishes her family life with her two sons, husband, and golden doodle.
