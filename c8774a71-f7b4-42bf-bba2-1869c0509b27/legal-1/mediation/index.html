Resolving Divorce Disputes Through Mediation

Divorce disputes can turn heated and destructive, but they don't have to be that way. Divorce mediation allows disputing spouses to find common ground and reach amicable solutions to their legal disputes.

At Zettersten & Hammond, we encourage our clients to work towards a settlement in mediation. We work with many talented local mediators to help you work through this process in an attempt to avoid protracted litigation. We can also help you explore other dispute resolution options.

Is Mediation Better?

Divorcing couples are required to try mediation, though it may not be successful in all cases. But for many, it proves the most effective means of achieving an amicable and lasting resolution to difficult disputes. Among its many advantages, mediation:

  • Puts more control in your hands – divorcing spouses and their attorneys come up with their own solutions, rather than letting a family court judge decide
  • Gets things right the first time – when people solve problems themselves, they are less likely to revisit issues in the future
  • Remains private, unlike a litigated divorce that becomes part of the public record
  • Is typically less expensive than traditional litigation

We work with many skilled divorce and custody mediators and can represent you through the process.
