About Catherine

Attorney and Mediator

Catherine Eaton is an attorney who has been practicing family law for over 20 years along with being the Mediator in numerous family cases. Her experience as a mediator makes her uniquely qualified to help clients navigate the legal process by providing creative and desirable solutions.

Catherine has successfully represented high profile clients in both Nashville and West Palm Beach while keeping the privacy of her clients. Raising three children as a single mother, Catherine understands how stressful divorce and children's issues can be on a client. It is her goal to help clients protect their children, their financial security and plan for their future.
