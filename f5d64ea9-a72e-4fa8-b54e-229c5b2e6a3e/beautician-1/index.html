/ about

Beauty Revival Barn is a luxury boutique med spa in Liberty Hill, Texas specializing in natural-looking injectables, facial balancing, and advanced skin rejuvenation. Every treatment is performed by a Registered Nurse and certified aesthetic injector, blending medical expertise with an artistic eye for subtle, beautiful results.

We combine expert, nurse-led care with a warm, Texas-chic atmosphere so you can look refreshed, radiant, and confidently yourself — never frozen or overdone.

Our clients come to us for personalized treatment plans, honest education, and long-term aesthetic care that puts safety, artistry, and natural beauty first.

Cecilia Wheeler, BSN, RN

Cecilia Wheeler created Beauty Revival Barn because she believes aesthetics should feel personal, honest, and empowering. After years working as a Registered Nurse in high-acuity emergency care, she saw how powerful it was when someone truly felt seen, safe, and cared for.

Cici specializes in subtle, confidence-boosting treatments that help women look like the best version of themselves, not someone else. She is known for her conservative, artistic approach, always prioritizing facial balance, natural movement, and long-term skin health.
