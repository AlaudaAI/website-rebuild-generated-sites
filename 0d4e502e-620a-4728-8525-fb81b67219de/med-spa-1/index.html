the blooming day spa experience

At Blooming Day Spa, we pride ourselves on providing a unique and exclusive experience tailored just for you. As our only guest, you can fully immerse yourself in relaxation and tranquility. Located in the San Marcos Historic District near downtown, our cozy craftsman style studio is designed to keep you relaxed and at peace. Potted plants brim over the front porch and the shelves inside. Soothing music, and filtered light create the calming atmosphere you have been searching for close to home.

During your visit, we pamper you with mostly local, organic bath and beauty products in our massage and body treatments, ensuring you leave feeling both refreshed and rejuvenated
