POLICIES

  • All Deposits are non-refundable.
  • Taxes and applicable fees are not included in listed prices.
  • We accept credit/debit cards, cash, and mobile payments (like Apple Pay, Venmo, Cashapp, Zelle, and Paypal) for your convenience!
  • Need to cancel? We kindly ask for 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule.
  • We offer a 10-minute grace period. If you're running later than that, we may need to adjust your appointment to ensure every guest receives the exceptional service they deserve.
  • A no-show/late reschedule occurs when an appointment is missed/rescheduled without letting us know in advance (before 24 hours). To ensure fairness to our team and fellow clients, a no-show/late reschedule fee of $50 will apply.
  • Once a service is complete, refunds are not available, so please don't hesitate to share any concerns during your appointment.
  • We're committed to providing an exceptional service experience. If you're not completely satisfied, simply let us know within 24 hours, and we'll work with you to find a potential solution that meets your needs.
