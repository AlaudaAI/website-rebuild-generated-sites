General

Is a consultation required before I book a treatment?

Not necessarily! We provide consultations at the start of each service to ensure it's safe and suitable for you.

Who will be performing my treatments?

All our treatments are performed by licensed medical professionals, including registered nurses who have specialized training in aesthetics. You're in expert hands!

How long can I expect Botox and fillers to last?

Botox usually lasts 3-4 months. Dermal fillers can last anywhere from 6-18 months, depending on the product and the area treated.

What kinds of fillers do you provide?

We offer a range of premium dermal fillers like the RHA Collection, Revanesse Versa, and Restylane. Whether you want to smooth fine lines, add volume, or enhance your facial contours, we'll help you choose the best option during your consultation.
