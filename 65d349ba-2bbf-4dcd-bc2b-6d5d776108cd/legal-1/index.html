About Everette Law Firm

Who is M. Katherine "Kathy" Everette?

Over the last 20+ years Kathy Everette has become an advocate for fair spousal treatment, and the go to attorney in Nashville for ALL of your families needs. She has a more than 90% settlement rate for divorces, because she listens and understands the personal and financial ramifications of a divorce.

Kathy earned an Accounting Degree from David Lipscomb University. She then went on to get her JD from Nashville School of Law, while working in Nashville's thriving Banking Industry as a Corporate Lending Officer. She spent over a decade working directly with some of Nashville's largest businesses as they expanded and helped to grow our city.
