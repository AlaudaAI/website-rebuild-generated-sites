Experienced.

Though my office is situated in Ashland City, I regularly appear in other Middle Tennessee courts, including Davidson, Dickson, Montgomery, and Robertson. I provide dedicated, thoughtful representation tailored to each client's situation. I successfully handle matters in General Sessions, Probate, and Juvenile courts.

Cases include probate and estate administration, uncontested divorces, custody and parentage matters, wills and estate planning, and misdemeanor defense. Having been admitted to the bar in 2010, I am capable and ready to put my experience to work for you.

Competent.

It is said that knowledge is power. This is true in law as well. Knowledge of the law AND knowledge of the facts. Many times you might see an attorney who knows the law but does not truly know his or her client and the facts that led them to court. Sometimes, though, you may have a passionate representative who knows every detail of your case but does not know how to apply the law. I can do both. I will listen and learn the facts better than anyone else involved in the case, while also knowing and explaining the law to you in terms you can understand. I will give you an honest evaluation of your case and let you know your options.
