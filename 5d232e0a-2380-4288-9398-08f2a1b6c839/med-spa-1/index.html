What to Expect

Therapists

Our therapists are here to make you feel welcome and comfortable. We don't mind questions so if there is something you need or don't see, please let us know.

Appointments

You may make an appointment up to three (3) months prior and before two (2) hours before the preferred time. Please note our cancellation policy.

Dress

At Royal Bliss, your comfort is our mission. We recommend wearing loose-fitting, comfortable clothes or just wearing what you feel the most relaxed in. We will provide a terry cloth wrap for Facial services. For Massage and Body services, underclothes are not necessary, however, if you prefer, undergarments may be worn. Please keep in mind that our therapists are trained to protect your privacy at all times.

Gratuity

We believe that a gratuity is a gesture of thanks for excellent service. You may add a percentage of choice at the end of the session or cash.

Cancellations

To serve all of our wonderful clients promptly at affordable prices, we reserve the right to charge 50% for appointments not canceled or rescheduled 24 hours in advance. Please get in touch with us if the need to cancel arises.

Location

We are located in Liberty Hill, Texas, right off Hwy 29 in a strip mall, next to a Bridal Shop. We have plenty of parking, so please stop by to make an appointment, shop the boutique, or just say hello!

No Show

To anyone that forgets their appointment or consciously decides to skip their appointment is considered a "no show". You will be charged the full amount of the service.

Products

We are proud to carry an extensive line of products. We use only organic, gentle products that specialize in corrective skin care for all skin types and ethnicities.

Minors

In accordance with the state of Texas, minors under the age of 18 must be accompanied by an adult to receive any service from Royal Bliss Med Spa.
