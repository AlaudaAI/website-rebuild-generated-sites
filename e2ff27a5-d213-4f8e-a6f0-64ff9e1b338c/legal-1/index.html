About the Firm

Life can be tough.

And not having proper legal representation during a lawsuit can make it a lot tougher. Whether it's divorce, child custody or support actions, orders of protection, post-divorce modifications, criminal allegations, or numerous other potential legal issues, the emotional toll of these sometimes necessary events can be overwhelming even before considering the legal process. We believe that no one should go through that alone and it is our job to be the support, resource, and advocate necessary to get our clients through these trying times.

Our priorities are hard work, professionalism, civility, and diligence in advocating for our clients as we accomplish what must be done -- together.
