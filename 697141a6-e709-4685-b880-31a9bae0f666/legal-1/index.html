About GNAA

Supporting the Rental Housing Industry

The Greater Nashville Apartment Association (GNAA), founded in 1968, supports the rental housing industry in Nashville and Middle Tennessee through our 4 core tenets: Advocacy • Communication • Education • Outreach


GNAA represents more than 174,000 units managed by companies as well as more than 430 supplier member businesses that provide products and services to GNAA members.


GNAA is an affiliate of both the National Apartment Association and the Tennessee Apartment Association.
