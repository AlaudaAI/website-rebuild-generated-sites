002 — who we are

A local and woman-owned business, Mint and Thyme first began serving our Austin community in 2014.

We pride ourselves on treatments that are both relaxing and effective, stressing the importance of educating our clients to properly care for their skin and help them achieve their skincare goals through professional treatments and a customized at-home regimen. We offer a range of services including clinical facials, hydrafacials, chemical peels, microneedling, photofacials, laser hair removal, Geneo, and cosmetic injections.

At Mint and Thyme we hold strongly to three core philosophies...

  • Lifelong skincare is a necessity, not a luxury; therefore, we make it accessible and affordable.
  • Internal balance creates external beauty.
  • We believe in taking a personal approach to each individual.
