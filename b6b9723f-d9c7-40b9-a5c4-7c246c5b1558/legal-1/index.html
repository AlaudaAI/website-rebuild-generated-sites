About Our Law Firm

Protecting Your Assets and Your Relationship with Your Children

The protection and preservation of your assets and your relationship with your children are of paramount importance to Lindsey Amonette McCarter Beauchamp + Glassford, PLLC. We provide strategic, goal oriented representation that draws on decades of advocacy and experience, both inside and outside the courtroom.

Service 01

Family Law

Provides unparalleled legal representation that includes unfettered access to legal counsel and step-by-step guidance through stressful, difficult, and important family law matters with focus on protection and preservation of assets and relationship with children

Service 02

Collaborative Law

Alternative dispute resolution process that utilizes financial, mental health, and child specialist neutrals as needed in addition to collaboratively trained legal counsel to assist in settlement discussion and negotiation process

Service 03

Mediation

Alternative dispute resolution in which a neutral mediator facilitates settlements, providing more control of the outcome of family law matters

Service 04

Estate Planning & Probate

Assists in securing your legacy and providing for loved ones through creation of Last Wills and Testaments, Durable Powers of Attorney, Durable Healthcare Powers of Attorney, and Living Wills, plus probate and administration of estates, conservatorships and guardianships
