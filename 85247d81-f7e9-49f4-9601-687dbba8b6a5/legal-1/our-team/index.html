Our Team

We recognize the importance of a strong attorney-client relationship and build that relationship through constant and clear communication with our clients, knowledge of our client's goals and our personal attention in attaining those goals. Our clients stay informed and receive regularly updated information concerning the status of their matter. They enjoy the peace of mind that comes with professional, experienced representation and exceptional client service.

Our lawyers' roots run deep in Murfreesboro and Middle Tennessee as evidenced by the active part we play in the community we represent, holding leadership roles in community groups, charitable organizations, civic associations, bar associations and local governments. Our strong sense of community is reflected in the personal interest we take in each client.

John Rodgers

Founding Member

John W. Rodgers is a founding member of the firm. He specializes in commercial and residential real estate. John has over thirty-five years of experience representing buyers, sellers, developers, lenders, and investors in real estate transactions throughout middle Tennessee.

James Barger

Member

James "Jay" Barger has been a member and attorney with the firm since 2002. He focuses his practice in the areas of personal injury construction litigation, divorce, child custody, and Social Security Disability. He received his undergraduate degree from Middle Tennessee State University.

Loren Sanderson

Member

Loren A. Sanderson is our newest and first female member who focuses her practice on litigation in the area of family law, including divorce, child custody, child support, contempt, dependent neglect, and adoption. Loren also assists clients with criminal defense.

In Memoriam 1948 – 2016

David Kious, Founding Member

It is often stated that you can count on one hand the number of people who have significantly impacted your life; however, David had a remarkable capability of significantly impacting the lives of everyone he knew due to his compassion and dedication to help others, as well as his caring and generous ways.
