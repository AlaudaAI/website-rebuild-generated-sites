Comprehensive Medical Services

Acne & Rosacea Treatment for Adult and Teen Skin

Your ARC dermatologist or advanced practice clinician (APC) will help you decide on the best treatment plan for your acne.

ActiveFX™ Laser Skin Resurfacing

ActiveFX laser resurfacing helps reduce wrinkles and fine lines, softens deep wrinkles, and improves skin tone and texture.

Biopsies of Skin Lesions and Conditions

A skin biopsy is done by removing cells or skin samples from your body for laboratory examination. The skin samples are used to help diagnose skin conditions and determine a treatment plan for you.

Botox Treatment of Axillary Hyperhidrosis

Botox® Cosmetic is a a safe, non-surgical procedure used to treat excess sweating in the underarms.

BOTOX® Injections

Botox® Cosmetic treatment is a safe, non-surgical procedure that smoothes the wrinkles in motion on your face (i.e. frown lines and crow's feet).

Candela Vbeam® Laser Treatment

The Candela VBeam® is a versatile, non-invasive laser that treats a variety of skin conditions including rosacea and facial redness.

Care for Chronic Skin Conditions like Psoriasis, Eczema, Lupus

Your ARC dermatologist or advanced practice clinician (APC) works with you to provide care to help ease chronic skin conditions like psoriasis, eczema, lupus, etc.

chemical peels

Chemical peels are used to treat rough, discolored, sun-damaged skin and acne and improve fine lines.

Diagnosis & Management of Cancer and Other Skin Lesions

Detecting severe skin conditions early can improve your quality of life. Our ARC dermatologists and advanced practice clinicians (APCs) can provide the proper care for skin cancer and other skin lesions.

Dysport® Injections

Dysport® is a prescription injection that helps temporarily soften and improve the look of frown lines.

Evaluation, Diagnosis, and Treatment of Bacterial/Viral/Fungal Infections

An ARC dermatologist or advanced practice clinician (APC) can evaluate, diagnose, and treat bacterial, viral, and fungal infections.

Evaluation, Diagnosis, and Treatment of Hair Loss and Nail Disorders

Find out what is causing your hair loss and nail disorders and receive treatment to get them back to a healthy state.

FemTouch™ Vaginal Rejuvenation

FemTouch vaginal rejuvenation is a non-surgical laser treatment for vaginal and urinary health, especially for those experiencing changes due to pre-menopause, post-menopause, and childbirth.

hydrafacial

Hydrafacial treatments cleanse, exfoliate, and hydrate your skin to improve the look of your skin tone and elasticity. Results are seen after only one treatment.

Intramuscular and Intralesional Injections

Dermatologic therapy injections that deliver medication deep into the muscle or skin lesions.

ipl photofacials

ARC offers IPL photofacials, a laser treatment, to treat sun damage, acne, symptoms of rosacea, age spots, freckles, veins, and other skin blemishes.

JUVÉDERM® Injections

JUVÉDERM® injectable gel is a great dermal filler that delivers nutrients to the cheeks, lips, and superficial wrinkles on your skin. Rejuvenate your skin.

Legend M²™ Microneedling with Radio Frequency

Microneedling helps rejuvenate facial collagen with little to no downtime. This procedure also helps skin conditions caused by acne scarring, wrinkles, stretch marks, pigment issues, and pore sizes.

medical aesthetics

ARC Medical Aesthetics offers safe, affordable, and effective medical aesthetic services using state-of-the-art equipment. You can receive the latest medical and cosmetic dermatology services in a relaxed and reassuring environment.

Mole Evaluations and Skin Cancer Screenings

The evaluation of suspicious moles and skin areas can help detect skin cancer and chronic conditions.

NuEra® Skin Tightening

NuEra® Tight uses radio frequency to tighten loose or sagging skin. This skin tightening treatment can also be used to reduce excess fat and cellulite.

Patch Testing for Contact Dermatitis

Contact dermatitis is a reaction that happens after your skin comes in direct contact with certain substances. Patch testing can be done to find the substance that's causing the rash.

photodynamic therapy

Photodynamic therapy (PDT) is a topical therapy-based treatment for pre-cancerous lesions and acne. A topical solution is applied to the affected area, which is absorbed by cancerous cells and sebaceous glands. Once absorbed, a specialized blue light is shown to the area, activating the solution, destroying the abnormal/damaged cancer cells, and shrinking sebaceous glands. The entire treatment is completed within 30 minutes.

piqo4 laser treatment

PiQo4 laser is a breakthrough laser technology that delivers outstanding results for skin rejuvenation, skin resurfacing, and tattoo removal.

platelet-rich fibrin

Platelet-Rich Fibrin (PRF), also known as a vampire facial, is an advanced biological treatment used to rejuvenate your skin and promote hair growth.

Restylane® Injections

Restylane® is used to treat superficial lines and wrinkles. Restore volume and fullness to the skin to correct facial wrinkles and folds.

ResurFX™ Skin Rejuvenation

Lumenis® ResurFX™ laser treatment is a fractional skin rejuvenation solution for younger, healthier, glowing looking skin. Promote deep collagen regeneration.

RF Microneedling with Platelet-Rich Fibrin (PRF)

RF Microneedling with Platelet-Rich Fibrin is an advanced aesthetic procedure that improves skin complexion, stimulates tissue regeneration, and promotes collagen production.

Sclerotherapy for Varicose and Spider Veins

Sclerotherapy treats spider veins, and may also treat other conditions including aching, burning, swelling and night cramps.

Sculptra® Injectable Filler

Sculptra® poly-L-lactic acid is an injectable dermal filler that rejuvenates the skin, reduces wrinkles and facial lines. Cosmetic treatment to promote collagen.

SmoothGlo™ Laser Skin Resurfacing

SmoothGlo™ is a laser resurfacing skin treatment that targets the tone, texture, and volume of your skin, addressing all three aspects in a single session.

Splendor X Laser Hair Removal

ARC offers Splendor X's laser hair removal system to permanently remove unwanted hair from the face, leg, arm, underarm, bikini, and other areas.

totalfx fractional laser

TotalFX Fractional CO2 Laser Skin Resurfacing tightens and rejuvenates the skin with two laser technologies. Treat scarring, stretch marks, wrinkles, and more.
