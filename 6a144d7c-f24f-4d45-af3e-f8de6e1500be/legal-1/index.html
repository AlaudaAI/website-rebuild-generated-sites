About Our Firm

Nashville Criminal Defense & Personal Injury Lawyers

At Nicholson & Hall PLLC, we provide more than just legal representation; we offer trusted guidance, personalized attention, and strong advocacy when you need it most. Based in Nashville, our firm has built a reputation across Middle Tennessee for delivering results in serious criminal and personal injury cases. Our firm practices in Davidson County and the surrounding counties. This includes: Davidson, Cheatham, Robertson, Rutherford, Sumner, Wilson, and Williamson Counties. With over 16 years of trial experience, we deeply understand the law, the courtroom, and the real-life challenges our clients face.

We are proud to be known for our compassionate, nonjudgmental approach. Whether you are facing criminal charges or recovering from a devastating injury, we meet you where you are—with respect, without assumptions, and with a clear focus on protecting your future. Our mission is to provide a personalized strategy grounded in experience, straightforward communication, and results.

Our commitment to service extends beyond the courtroom. We are actively involved in the Nashville community, including our work with Dismas House, which supports successful reentry for formerly incarcerated individuals. We believe in second chances and the power of community-based change. This spirit of commitment informs how we practice law and treat every client who walks through our doors.

We are also proud to provide bilingual services for our Spanish-speaking clients. Everyone deserves access to justice, regardless of language or background. Our firm offers culturally competent, Spanish-language support to help clients feel understood, respected, and empowered.
