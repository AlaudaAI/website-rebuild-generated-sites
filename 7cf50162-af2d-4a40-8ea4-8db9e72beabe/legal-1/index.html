Divorce

The goal in a divorce is to hopefully resolve it without going to trial, because trial is costly, stressful, and uncertain. But if you do go to trial, Brenda's goal is to be an aggressive advocate on your behalf and fight for you to get a positive result.

Child Custody and Parenting Plans

Disputes over legal custody of a child are about which parent has the legal right to make decisions about a child's upbringing. Brenda cares deeply about making sure the best interests of a child are enacted.

Child Support Modification and Enforcement

If one parent has a significant change in income, or a child's needs drastically change, the amount of child support may need to be modified. Brenda can help you take legal action to compel payment.

Wills

A Will is a legal document that reflects your wishes about what should happen to your assets and property after you have passed away. Brenda will help you make sure your Will is valid, clear, legally sound, and will ensure your wishes are followed correctly.

Probate

Probate is the court-supervised process every Will goes through after the person who made it passes away that determines if the Will is valid and legally sound and then acts according to the Will's wishes.

Family Mediation

Mediation helps people resolve their disputes without going through the stress of a contested court case, the time that it takes, and the money that it costs. Brenda has a good track record of settling cases.
