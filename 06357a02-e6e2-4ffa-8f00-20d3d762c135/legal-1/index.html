Welcome to GSRM Law

Comprehensive Legal Experience in Nashville

Located in Nashville, Tennessee, the law firm of Gullett Sanford Robinson & Martin PLLC (GSRM Law) provides its clients with a team of 29 lawyers who collaborate across legal disciplines to serve a wide array of needs for businesses and individual clients in Nashville, the state of Tennessee, and across the country. GSRM Law is a reliable partner for navigating the intricacies of the legal system — offering legal advice, personalized attention, and steadfast representation. The firm has succeeded for more than 80 years because it has remained committed to its approach, its attorneys and employees, and its values.

Bankruptcy, Restructuring and Insolvency

Commercial entities and business owners facing economic crises, including bankruptcy and reorganization, workouts and turnarounds, creditors' rights and remedies, debtor-in-possession matters, bankruptcy litigation and state and federal receiverships

Business and Corporate

Commercial dealings ranging from organizing business entities, handling mergers, acquisitions and dispositions to structuring commercial transactions, drafting employment contracts, and counseling clients on matters relating to real property

Business Succession Planning

Strategic investment that advances the sustainability and growth of privately held and family-owned businesses with succession plans that minimize disruptions to company operations, maximize tax advantages, and assuage potential conflict among family members

Estate Planning and Probate

Estate planning, estate and trust administration, conservatorships, and estate related litigation for individuals, families, fiduciaries and businesses

Family Law

Comprehensive range of family law services including premarital and postnuptial agreements, domestic partnership agreements, marital dissolution agreements, divorce and separation, property and debt division, juvenile court matters, parentage, child custody and support, spousal support, and collaborative law

Labor and Employment

All areas of employment law in a variety of sectors including government, non-profits, and business throughout Tennessee and across the country, from creation of sound employment policies and procedures to defense of complex class action lawsuits

Litigation

Courtroom results and innovative strategies that advance business interests, protect client holdings and block potential loss, including negotiating resolutions outside of court and complex, persistent litigation, trial or appellate counsel

Mediation

Dispute resolution through mediation process with six attorneys certified as Tennessee Supreme Court Rule 31 Mediators to assist with the settlement of disputes

Real Estate

All real estate matters including litigation and dispute resolution, acquisition and sale, leasing and development, borrowing, lending and RESPA, bankruptcy and reorganization, and zoning and land use planning

Tax Planning

Federal and state tax services to large and small businesses, nonprofit organizations and individuals to minimize employment taxes and taxation of business and personal income while efficiently complying with federal and state tax reporting and payment requirements
