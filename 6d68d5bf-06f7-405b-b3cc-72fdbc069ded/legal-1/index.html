What We Do

Our Practice Areas

IMMIGRATION LAW

Today's complex federal immigration system requires knowledgeable counsel to guide individuals and families through every stage of the process. Tennessee Law Group provides immigration law services, representing clients before immigration courts and federal agencies in matters including: Family-Based Immigration – Adjustment of Status – Asylum – Deportation Defense – Naturalization & Citizenship – Visas & Waivers.

CRIMINAL LAW

Nothing in the law is more important than your freedom. Please contact us as soon as you or someone you love is under investigation or charged with a crime. Never comment on any aspect of a charge until you have the opportunity to consult a lawyer. Criminal offenses include: DUI – Drug Offenses – Domestic Assault – Assault & Battery – Theft.

FAMILY LAW

Family Law is a term that encompasses most legal issues surrounding families. These issues include: Marriage – Divorce – Adoption – Termination of Parental Rights – Child Advocacy – Child support – Child custody

PERSONAL INJURY LAW

The field of tort law is a major area of law that encompasses a civil wrong, intentional or accidental involving: Automobile Accidents – Civil Rights – Wrongful Death – Governmental Tort Liability – Medical Malpractice
