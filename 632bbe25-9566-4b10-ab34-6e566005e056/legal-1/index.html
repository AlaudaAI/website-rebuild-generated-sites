About Our Firm

Welcome

You usually need an attorney only at the more difficult life moments such as a divorce, personal injury, a broken contract, fraud or a child custody issue. These are emotional events and you need someone you can trust to navigate the legal challenges successfully to help you return to normal life as soon as possible.

That is what you will find at The Rushton Law Firm, PLLC. Our attorneys are experienced in divorce, child custody, orders of protection, alimony, child support and other domestic relations matters. Our attorneys listen and strive to understand your situation, how the law will apply to it, and zealously act in your best interests. You are not alone.

The Rushton Law Firm, PLLC is a full-service law firm, caring for and serving clients in Davidson County, Tennessee and surrounding counties. Call us today for Nashville divorce and child custody attorneys at 615-200-7239 or contact us online for a consultation with an attorney regarding your case.
