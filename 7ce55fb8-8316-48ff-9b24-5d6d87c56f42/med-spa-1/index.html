002 — about lux skin spa

Lux Skin Spa is a boutique skincare studio in Cedar Park, TX, specializing in advanced, result-driven treatments with a refined, calming approach. Combining modern technology with professional-grade skincare, creating customized facials designed to address aging, sun damage, rosacea, dehydration, and compromised skin barriers and acne.

Every complexion is unique, which is why every treatment begins with a personalized consultation to ensure your skin receives exactly what it needs. With over 20 years of expertise, I take a thoughtful, detail-oriented approach to every service blending corrective techniques with a relaxing, restorative experience.

A strong healthy barrier is the foundation of beautiful skin. Many common concerns like dryness, sensitivity, inflammation, and premature aging skin stem from a weakened barrier. My treatments focus on resurfacing, deeply hydrating and restoring balance so your skin can function at best and truly glow.

At Lux Skin Spa, you'll experience the perfect balance of results and relaxation. Whether your goal is to smooth fine lines, improve tone and texture, brighten dull skin, or simply take time to reset, each facial is designed to leave you feeling refreshed, confident and radiant.
