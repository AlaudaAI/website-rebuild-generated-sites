welcome to austex wellness & medical spa

Established in 2016, our Lakeway practice, founded by Laurel and David Belfiore, is committed to pioneering the future of aesthetic treatments and wellness. Our offerings include the latest in skincare and rejuvenation technologies such as HydraFacial Syndeo, DAXXIFY, Vivace Ultra RF Microneedling, Clarity II Laser Hair Removal, and Exceed Microneedling. Each technology is selected for its proven effectiveness and ability to deliver outstanding results, ensuring that we bring tomorrow's beauty advances to you today.

With a team of experts, including our Medical Director, Nurse Practitioner, and seasoned aestheticians, we deliver an exceptional experience that merges luxury with the utmost professionalism. Each member contributes over a decade of expertise, ensuring unparalleled service that's consistently recognized through our five-star client reviews.

Serving Lakeway, Bee Cave, Spicewood, and the broader Austin Hill Country community, we are committed to helping you 'Renew Your Health and Beauty'.
