Welcome

Hello, I'm Thomas Jay Martin, JR. Attorney At Law and I provide legal counsel in Tennessee. Thomas Jay Martin, JR. Attorney At Law was founded in 1991 and I currently serve the Sumner County area. I've been providing legal counsel for over 20 years and I'm a member of the Tennessee bar association. I'm a former Tennessee administrative law judge and I was appointed Special Judge for Sumner County General Sessions, Circuit Court, and Juvenile Court. My legal services include injuries, workers compensation, misdemeanors, felonies, social security, family law and much more. For a full list of services, visit my legal services page.
