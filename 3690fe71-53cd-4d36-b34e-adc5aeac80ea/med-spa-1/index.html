Will I Have Scars?

If you're considering getting a BBL in Austin, you've probably been doing your homework. If so, you already know that during the procedure, your doctor will remove fat from specified areas of your body and then reinject it into your buttocks in several places. It's easy to learn the basics of the procedure, but harder to find information on what type of scarring you'll have after all this surgical poking and prodding.

The answer is very little. The fat inserted into your buttocks is implanted through a syringe. As a result, you really shouldn't notice any scars on your backside. Think of it just like getting a flu shot. You may, however, notice minimal scarring at the liposuction sites where your doctor removed the donor fat. These are typically only 2 to 3 millimeters long and not highly visible or obvious since our surgeons are quite skilled at hiding them in the natural contours of your body whenever possible. They won't be completely invisible, however. In 3 to 6 months, these scars will fade from pink to a more natural skin color and many patients have trouble finding them at all.
