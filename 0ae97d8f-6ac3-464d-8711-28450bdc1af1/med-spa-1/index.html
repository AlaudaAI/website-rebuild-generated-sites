What Exactly does BOTOX do?
BOTOX is an FDA-approved neuromodulator used for cosmetic treatment that utilizes botulinum neurotoxins to reduce the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. Our Austin BOTOX treatment is among one of the most popular aesthetic treatments in Austin, TX. With over 4 million Americans getting BOTOX every year, it's the leading cosmetic treatment in the world. BOTOX has many uses, but it's most popular for reducing fine lines & wrinkles on the forehead, crow's feet, and between the eyebrows.
What type of Dermal Filler should I receive?
The type of dermal filler you should use greatly depends on where you want to add volume. The most popular dermal fillers include Juvederm, Restylane, Voluma, and Radiesse. Many of our Austin Lip Fillers patients desire more volume in the lips. For lip injections, we typically use Restylane. Juvederm is another very popular filler for our patients, sometimes used in the lips and other times in the cheeks and jawline.
What's the Best Treatment in Austin for Wrinkles?
Depending on where the wrinkles are and the severity of the wrinkles, we'll likely have different recommendations. The best way to get the most accurate information is to schedule a consultation at our Austin, TX Medical Spa. With multiple locations around Austin, our team will sit down with you and give you the benefits of each treatment. Popular non-surgical procedures for wrinkles include BOTOX, Morpheus8, and IPL PhotoFacial.
