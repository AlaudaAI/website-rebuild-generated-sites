001 — about relive health

At Relive Health Austin, we believe symptoms are signals, pointing to something deeper. Our team takes a personalized approach by looking at your full health picture: lifestyle, lab results, concerns, and goals. This helps us identify the root causes behind what's keeping you from feeling your best.

Whether you're dealing with low energy, weight gain, hormone imbalance, or skin issues, we treat your health with a whole-body perspective. Gut health, stress, sleep, nutrition, and physical activity all play a role in your day-to-day wellbeing. That's why we offer everything from comprehensive lab testing and hormone therapy to IV vitamin drips and aesthetic services, all designed to help you look and feel better from the inside out.

Relive Health Austin combines experience in functional medicine, aesthetics, and wellness with a commitment to long-term care. When you walk through our doors, you're not just a patient, you're a partner on a journey toward lasting health and confidence.

Meet Our Team

Brian Brown, MD
Medical Director
Dr. Brian Brown was born and raised in Corpus Christi, Tx. He went to college at Texas Tech University where he earned a degree in biology. He then went on to attend Medical School at the American University of the Caribbean. He trained in various locations across the country such as Baltimore, NYC and multiple cities throughout the state of Texas. Upon graduation he returned to Corpus Christi and completed his residency.
