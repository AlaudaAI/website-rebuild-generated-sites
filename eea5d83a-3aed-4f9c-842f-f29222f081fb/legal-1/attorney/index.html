Thomas H. Bray

Attorney

Attorney Thomas H. Bray has been practicing law since 2009. As a solo practitioner his practice is diverse, handling family law, personal injury, and criminal cases. Thomas H. Bray's experience is in the courtroom. Years of experience in both criminal law, Divorce and Personal Injury in front of Judges in Rutherford and the surrounding counties. He's an aggressive litigator who is present, passionate and proactive for his clients. He works closely with his clients to explain the law, so that his clients can make informed decisions. This Rutherford county attorney is committed to achieving the best outcome for his clients.

Background:

I am originally from Jackson Tennessee. I moved from West Tennessee to Middle Tennessee to attend Middle Tennessee State University where I graduated in 2005.

I began my legal career in the Rutherford County Chancery Court as a Deputy Clerk. I later served as law clerk to the Honorable J. Mark Rogers, Circuit Judge for the 16th Judicial District.

After leaving the Judge's office I worked in office of Bragg, Mansfield, and Stegall where I assisted with civil, criminal, and real property cases, while attending Nashville School of Law.

My current practice at Bennett, Michael & Bray is devoted to Divorce, Family Law, and Criminal Defense cases. I practice regularly in the Chancery, Circuit, Juvenile and Child Support Courts of Rutherford and surrounding Middle Tennessee counties.
