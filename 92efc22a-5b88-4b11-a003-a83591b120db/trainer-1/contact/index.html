Alpine Adventures is an established outdoor recreation leader in Washington State. Join us to experience what thousands of satisfied customers do every year, the fulfillment of your rafting desires. Whether on a scenic float or a whitewater adventure, rest assured you'll be well taken care of and enjoy your time with Alpine Adventures.

Complete the form below and we'll contact you to let you know about trip options. During peak rafting season April-August you can expect a response right away.

In Good Company...

Everybody is comfortable around us including families, corporations, church groups, boy scouts & girl scouts, even your group of friends. You choose – thrilling chutes over cascading waterfalls, healthy and challenging whitewater, or relaxing floats through spectacular scenery. An average group size for us is about 35 people. We can accommodate groups of well over 100, but also enjoy spending time with smaller groups of guests.

In Good Hands...

We're committed to your safety and satisfaction. Share the camaraderie with our Professional Guide Corps, who know and love these waters. Our perfect safety record results from extensive training (including Swiftwater Rescue techniques) and thousands of river miles. Our professional equipment features Sotar and Aire self-bailing rafts, oar guided craft when requested, Coast Guard Type V P.F.D.'s (personal floatation devices; life jackets), helmets, splash jackets, and clean wetsuits & booties for all sizes.

We are Washington State Licensed and members of:

  • Washington Outfitters and Guides Association (WOGA) – www.woga.org
  • America Outdoors – (AO) – www.americaoutdoors.org
  • Plus over a dozen local chambers of commerce and visitor bureaus.

Reservation & Payment Guidelines

Please make your rafting reservations as soon as possible to ensure the best experience with the least amount of hassle. If you are unsure how many people to book your trip for, make a reservation for the minimum number of confirmed people in your group, then add additional members as they are confirmed. The total payment is due 10 days in advance of your scheduled rafting trip (or upon making the reservation if less than 10 days in advance). Last minute reservations should call at least one day in advance to make sure a spot is available in a raft on the river you'd like to run. Walk-ins are welcome but can not be guaranteed. In order to confirm a trip, a deposit of 50% of the total trip cost is required at the time of booking. Visa and MasterCard accepted. If preferred, you may send a check or money order to: Alpine Adventures, P.O. Box 373, Gold Bar, WA 98251.

Refund Policies

A 100% refund can be processed (minus transaction fees) if you cancel your rafting trip at least 30 days in advance of the booked trip date. A 50% refund can be processed (minus transaction fees) if you cancel 10-29 days in advance. No refund will be processed if you cancel less than 10 days prior to your trip or if you don't show up on the day of your trip. If fewer people show up on the trip date, you will be held accountable for all reserved spots. We raft rain or shine!
