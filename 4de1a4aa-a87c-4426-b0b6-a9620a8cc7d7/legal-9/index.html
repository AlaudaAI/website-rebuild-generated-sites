Tips if you're arrested in Dickson

Whether you've been arrested before or not, the law has provisions meant to protect you. It's in the Constitution, so those rights are not negotiable. The law offers protections if you're arrested, but you need to know how to use them.

One of the most important things to remember is to remain silent. Next, you need to ask for an attorney. Don't mince your words. Say loud and clear, "I want my lawyer."

Don't speak until you have had a chance to talk to your attorney and have them there to advise you.
